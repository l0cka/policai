Policai is an Australian AI Policy Tracker that aggregates, analyses, and visualises AI policy, regulation, and governance developments across federal and state/territory jurisdictions.

## What We Track

We monitor policy developments across all Australian jurisdictions — federal, state, and territory — covering legislation, regulation, guidelines, frameworks, and standards related to artificial intelligence.

## Stay Updated

Check back here for project updates, new data source announcements, and commentary on significant AI policy developments across Australia.
